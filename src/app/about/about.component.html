About Us

The History of Twinkle Kidz Preschool

Established in 2014, Twinkle Kidz Preschool provides education to the young children in Durbanville and surrounding areas situated near the winelands.We have consistently graduated children with a sound foundation in their formative years to other feeder schools in Durbanville, Cape Town & United Kingdom.

We provide a nurturing bilingual enviroment in English and Afrikaans.Where we encourage children,respect,skills and values that enable them to grow into confident children.

We do not have an entrance exam and are a fully inclusive preschool.We enrol children from 18 months - 6 yearsof age.

We offer a variety of extra mural programs. Our education curriculm is continously reviewed to ensure our we have children who realise and enjoy their education to their full benefit.

We welcome a visit to view our spacious classes and outdoor play area.
